Abstract

"Career Catalyst" is an innovative online job portal designed to simplify the recruitment process for both job seekers and employers. This platform allows job seekers to create profiles, upload resumes, and apply for jobs across various fields using advanced search capabilities. Employers can post job openings, review applications, and download resumes with ease. One of the key features of Career Catalyst is its AI-powered matching system, which ensures job seekers meet at least 50% of the skills required for a job before they can apply. This minimizes irrelevant applications and helps employers find qualified candidates faster. The admin oversees the portal's operations, managing the companies and job listings. The platform is accessible globally, breaking down geographical barriers and providing a seamless experience for both candidates and employers. By utilizing cutting-edge technologies, Career Catalyst revolutionizes job searching and recruitment, making it efficient and user-friendly for all involved.

End Users Job Seekers - First time candidates can sign up or existing candidates can sign in. After signing in, they can search for jobs according to their qualifications, apply to these positions and upload their resumes. They can also update their details at any point of time.

End Users Companies - Company can register itself with the portal and once approved by admin, can sign in to the portal. They can see if candidates have applied for their job positions posted. They can also post new jobs.
